Racism Around the World

The survey asked respondents in more than 80 different countries to identify kinds of people they would not want as neighbors.

Where racism could possibly occur
  • About 35% of people experience racism in public
  • 1 in 3 public work places experience racism
  • 1 in 5 experience racism in sport related activities
  • About 43% are commonly happening at school
  • About 33% are commonly encountered on the interwebs/online
